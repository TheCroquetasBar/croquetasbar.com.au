About us

Originally from Barcelona, Spain, The Croquetas Bar team is passionate for food and delighted to share our cuisine and culture with you. Javier, our experienced head-chef, has worked many years in Spanish tapas restaurants and follows the traditional recipes to get the authentic flavour.

We cater for every function you may have, from weddings, birthdays, or afterwork. We can help you make your event a remarkable experience with our authentic mobile Spanish tapas bar.
